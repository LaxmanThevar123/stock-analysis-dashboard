## Key Metrics to Watch

### Supply Chain Health
- **Single-source dependency**: >50% from one country = High Risk
- **Substitute availability**: Multiple options = Low Risk
- **Adoption timeline**: <2 years = Investable

### Investment Ratings
- **STRONG BUY**: Immediate substitute, high adoption
- **BUY**: Clear substitute path, 1-2 year timeline
- **ACCUMULATE**: Emerging substitute, 2-3 years
- **HOLD**: Uncertain substitute, >3 years
- **AVOID**: No substitute, high dependency
